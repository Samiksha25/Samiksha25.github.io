Crafts

The yellow orange dreamcatcher

Dreamcatcher as the name suggests catch bad dreams with their net-like structure and gently let the beautiful dreams fall with their feathers. Traditionally made with willow hoops and real feathers, were considered sacred.I enjoy weaving complex patterns, incorporating vibrant colors and beads in them. I have learnt how to be meticulous while experimenting with this art form.

Batman: String art

This art piece is an amalgamation of my favourite DC super hero Batman and a simple yet beautiful art form called String art. Ironically, I dedicate this artwork to the biggest super villain of all time "The Joker".

Painting

The long beard

Watercolor painting is one of the toughest painting techniques as it requires the use of minimal colour and water. This painting has been inspired by my art teacher's teacher who had a long beard and more wisdom than anyone I had ever met in my life.

Oil pastel painting: Dyaneshwar

I started my journey as a painter with oil pastels, much underestimated painting technique but it really helps enhance one's understanding in mixing colours. This painting was donated to my school as a tribute, and was made on a full size canvas.
